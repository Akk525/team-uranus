Saturn

Facts:

Uranus is very cold and windy. It is surrounded by a faint ring, and more than 25 moons. Uranus appears greenish-blue due to the large amounts of methane in its atmosphere, which absorbs red light, and reflects blue light.

Fun Facts:

  • Uranus has 27 moons.
  • Uranus was the first planet discovered with a telescope.
  • Uranus is the 7th planet from the Sun.
